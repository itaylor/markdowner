Log Entry: The One Where Lolla Never Docked

Ensigns, I messed up. I totally missed Lollapalooza '97. I was stuck in my parents' minivan running a level‑3 diagnostic on my Discman. I figured, “No biggie — I'll catch the '98 show when it swings back through Seattle.”
Computer, locate Lollapalooza 1998. Computer: <NULL>. That's right: no '98 tour. Somewhere in a parallel timeline, I'm crowd‑surfing to Tool and not wearing a wrist brace from coding.

Every night I set my alarm to Red Alert and stare at the ceiling like a malfunctioning holodeck. If anyone needs me, I'll be in my quarters, replicating sadness and playing Winamp skins that look like LCARS. It really whips the llama's, uh, starship.

System Report: Windows 98

Upgraded to Windows 98. Spoiler: it crashes more than a shuttlecraft flown by a redshirt on bring‑your‑tribble‑to‑work day. Blue Screen of Death? More like Blue Alert of Infinite Loops. Today it took down my Quake match, my ICQ window (uh-oh!), and my Angelfire upload — all in one photon torpedo.

Workaround: I keep a floppy with scanreg /restore like a lucky tricorder. If anyone knows how to set IRQs so my sound card stops pretending to be a cloaked Romulan, email me at jono@startreksad.com.
